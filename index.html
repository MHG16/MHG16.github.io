Star Wars the Travel Guide

Final Project at The Iron Yard, Austin, TX. I love Star Wars and who wouldnt love to travel to the exotic planets of that galaxy? This is a travel guide for the citizens of that galaxy a long time ago and far, far away. Site includes a galaxy map with 10 clickable planets. Each planet then has a separate page with information including a photo and a blog area for fan fiction. Site created with fan created Star Wars api: www.swapi.co.
